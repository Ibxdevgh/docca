1. Introduction

Welcome to DOCCA ("we," "our," or "us"). DOCCA is a Web3-native platform where users can create, customize, and own AI agents powered by ChatGPT, all backed by Solana NFTs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, including our website, services, and applications.

By using DOCCA, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Wallet Information

When you connect your Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom, Backpack), we collect your public wallet address. We do not have access to your private keys, seed phrases, or any other sensitive wallet information.

2.2 AI Agent Data

We collect information about your AI agents, including:

  • Agent traits (tone, purpose, personality characteristics)
  • Agent name and avatar
  • Chat history and interactions with your AI agent
  • Agent memory and evolution data
  • NFT metadata associated with your agent

2.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of DOCCA, including:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and general location data
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Error logs and performance data

2.4 Communication Data

If you contact us or sign up for beta access, we may collect your email address and any other information you provide in your communications with us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Authenticate your wallet and verify NFT ownership
  • Generate and personalize your AI agent's behavior and responses
  • Store and manage your agent's memory and chat history
  • Process NFT minting and manage on-chain metadata
  • Send you updates, notifications, and support communications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • OpenAI (for ChatGPT API integration)
  • IPFS storage providers (for NFT metadata)
  • Blockchain infrastructure providers (for Solana transactions)
  • Analytics and monitoring services

4.2 Blockchain Transparency

Information stored on the Solana blockchain, including your wallet address and NFT ownership, is publicly accessible and immutable. This is inherent to blockchain technology.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication using wallet signatures
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to blockchain immutability)
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below. Note that information stored on the blockchain cannot be deleted due to its immutable nature.

7. Third-Party Services

Our services integrate with third-party services, including:

  • Solana blockchain and wallet providers

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies to understand how they handle your information.

8. Children's Privacy

DOCCA is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.
